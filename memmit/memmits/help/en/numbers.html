Numbers

Memorizing large numbers or sequences of digits is not as hard as it seems. If you try to do it head on, you'll probably find that you can't remember more than 7-20 digits at a time, but there is an easier and more efficient approach.

Our brain is more comfortable with remembering images or objects than abstract numeric data. Because of this, memorizing numbers usually comes down to creating a sequence of images or a story, each element of which corresponds to one or more digits. To learn how to do this click the "simplify" icon in the lower left-hand corner.
